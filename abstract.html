abstract series

below are a series of photo collage experiments. they’re each composed of images taken throughout philadelphia. inspired by some of the work of martin venesky, it the experiments have a number of different rules to them: all the images are converted into single color screens, images can only be cropped at 90 degree angles (so no fancy masking, and once an image has been used in one collage it can’t appear in another collage.
